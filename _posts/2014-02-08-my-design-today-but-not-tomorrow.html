I've decided to change a number of things in my working design, which will likely entail changes to the windows, so I thought I'd post what it looks like right now, because soon it will be completely different.

The changes I'm making will make the design more flexible, with less built-ins. I try to think about who will use this house in the future. I'm designing it for me and my needs, but I'm also building it to outlive me, so I want it to be easily re-purposed.
